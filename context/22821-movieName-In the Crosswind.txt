In the Crosswind (Estonian: Risttuules) is a 2014 Estonian drama film directed by Martti Helde. It was screened in the Contemporary World Cinema section at the 2014 Toronto International Film Festival.[1] The film is about the forced deportation by Stalin's Russia of an Estonian family to Siberia in the June deportation.
