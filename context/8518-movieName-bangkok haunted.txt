Bangkok Haunted (Thai: ผีสามบาท) is a 2001 Thai horror film compilation, directed by Oxide Pang and Pisut Praesangeam. It is a trio of ghost stories, as told by three people sitting around in a darkened Bangkok bar.An antiques dealer discovers that a dancer's musical spirit possesses an old drum in her shop.A lonely young woman is given an aphrodisiac perfume. It is extracted from corpses.A police cadet searches for the truth behind a girl's suicide by hanging.At the time of its release in Thailand, Bangkok Haunted was the second-biggest grossing Thai horror film since Nang Nak in 1999.The film was screened at the London Thai Film Festival on October 13, 2002.
