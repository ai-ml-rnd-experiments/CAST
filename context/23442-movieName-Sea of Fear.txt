Sea of Fear is a 2006 film about a group of people on a boat who fall victim to a killing spree. Directed by Andrew Schuth, it was released on DVD on August 22, 2006. The film is rated PG-13 by the MPAA.Four friends take a vacation on a boat, with its skipper and three crew. By a campfire one night, the seven discuss the kind of death each fears most.
