Shaun the Sheep is a British claymation children's television series and a spin-off of the Wallace and Gromit franchise. The title character is Shaun (a sheep previously featured in the 1995 short film A Close Shave and the Shopper 13 short film from the 2002 Wallace and Gromit's Cracking Contraptions series). The series focuses on his adventures on a small farm as the leader of his flock.The series first aired in the UK on the CBBC channel on 5 March 2007 and has been broadcast in 180 countries. The series consists of 170 seven-minute episodes. The fifth series contains 20 episodes and was first aired in the Netherlands from 1 December 2015 until 1 January 2016[2][3][4] and in Australia on ABC Kids from 16 January 2016 until 1 May 2016. In the United States, a series of Shaun the Sheep shorts aired in between commercial breaks on Disney Channel starting on 8 July 2007. However, as of 2018, the series had not been aired on the network for a decade.
