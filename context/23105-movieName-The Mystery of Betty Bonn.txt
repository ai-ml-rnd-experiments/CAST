The Mystery of Betty Bonn (German: Das Geheimnis um Betty Bonn) is a 1938 German adventure film directed by Robert A. Stemmle and starring Maria Andergast, Theodor Loos and Hans Nielsen.[1] It was made by the leading German studio UFA, based on a novel by Friedrich Lindemann. A British attorney working on a small island wants to return home to London, but first has to solve a mysterious crime.This article related to a German film of the 1930s is a stub.
