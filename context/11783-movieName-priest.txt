The Priest is a 2021 Indian Malayalam-language supernatural horror mystery film directed by debutant Jofin T. Chacko,[2]which marks the first collaboration of Mammootty and Manju Warrier in their career.[3] Produced jointly by B. Unnikrishnan and Anto Joseph,[4] the film features original score and soundtrack composed and produced by Rahul Raj. The film was scheduled to release on 31 July 2020 on the occasion of Eid Al Adha,[5][6] but production was delayed due to the COVID-19 pandemic in India.[7] The film released on 11 March 2021[8] to mostly positive reviews with the performances and background score garnering unanimous praise.[9][10][11][12] The film streams on Amazon Prime.The Priest follows the adventures of Father Carmen Benedict.
