Broadway Nights is a 1927 American silent romantic drama film directed by Joseph C. Boyle and starring Lois Wilson and Sam Hardy. The film marked the debuts of Sylvia Sidney, as well as Barbara Stanwyck and Ann Sothern who had small roles as fan dancers.[1][2][3]Broadway Nights is considered to be a lost film.[3]This article about a silent romantic drama film is a stub.
