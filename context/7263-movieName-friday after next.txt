Friday After Next is a 2002 American stoner comedy film directed by Marcus Raboy and starring Ice Cube (who also wrote the film) and Mike Epps. It is the third installment in the Friday series. The film was theatrically released on November 22, 2002, to generally negative reviews. This film has since gained a cult following.The film begins when a robber, disguised as Santa Claus, breaks into Craig and Day-Day's apartment at 3:37 AM on Christmas Eve. Craig fights with the robber, trashing their apartment while making frantic but futile attempts to wake Day-Day. The robber escapes with Craig and Day-Day's Christmas presents and rent money. The same morning, the police come over to get a report.
