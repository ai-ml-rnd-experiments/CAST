The Holy Office (Spanish: El santo oficio) is a 1974 Mexican drama film directed by Arturo Ripstein. It was nominated for the Palme d'Or at the 1974 Cannes Film Festival[1] and won the Silver Goddess for Best Film in 1974 awarded by the Mexican Cinema Journalists.[2]This article related to a Mexican film of the 1970s is a stub.
