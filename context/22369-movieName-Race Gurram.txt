Race Gurram (transl. Race Horse) is a 2014 Indian Telugu-language action comedy film directed by Surender Reddy and produced by Nallamalupu Srinivas under his banner Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Productions.[5] The film stars Allu Arjun and Shruti Haasan in their first collaboration while S. Thaman composed the music.[6]Principal photography began on 13 May 2013 in Hyderabad[7] with the entire shoot completed on 22 February 2014.[8] The film released worldwide on 11 April 2014.[9] The film marked Allu Arjun's first ₹100 crore grosser[4] With a distributor's share of ₹57 crore, the film emerged as highest grossing Telugu film of that year.[10] It went on to bag Filmfare Awards for Best Actor, Best Actress and Best Playback Singer – Male.
