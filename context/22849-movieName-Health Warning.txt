Health Warning (alternative titles: Da Lei Tai, Da Lui Toi, Future Flash Kung Fu, Digital Master) is a 1983 Hong Kong film directed by Kirk Wong. It is a dystopian sci-fi kung fu film set in the future.[1]At the 3rd Hong Kong Film Awards, Health Warning was nominated for Best Picture, and Kirk Wong was nominated for Best Director.[2] The film was listed as one of the "Best 100 Chinese Motion Pictures" during the 24th Hong Kong Film Awards.This article related to a Hong Kong film of the 1980s is a stub.
