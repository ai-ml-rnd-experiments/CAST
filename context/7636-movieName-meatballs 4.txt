Meatballs 4 (also known as Meatballs 4: To the Rescue) is a 1992 film and the fourth and final installment in the Meatballs series of films. It was shot in its entirety at Bass Lake, California, in the late summer of 1991. Originally conceived as a comedy-drama titled Happy Campers, the project was retooled after production had begun to be part of the Meatballs series.[1]Ricky is the hottest waterskiing instructor around, and he has just been rehired by his former employer/camp to whip up attendance. However, the camp is in serious financial trouble and the owner of a rival, more popular, camp wants to buy them out.
