Man in Outer Space (Czech: Muž z prvního století) is a 1962 Czechoslovak science fiction comedy film directed by Oldřich Lipský. It was entered into the 1962 Cannes Film Festival.[1]This article related to a Czech film of the 1960s is a stub.
