The Dark Island is a six-part British television miniseries, produced by Gerard Glaister for the BBC. It premi√®red on 8 July 1962. It was later adapted for radio, which was transmitted in 1969. It was set on the Outer Hebridean island of Benbecula, though the majority of the series was filmed on South Uist.[citation needed]The plot involves the discovery of a mysterious torpedo found on the shore of Benbecula. A naval team descends on the area to deal with the torpedo accompanied by Nicolson, an intrigued security officer. Further investigation of the torpedo reveals an international spy kit, the contents of which include a Finnish passport, British and Swedish currency, and most intriguing of all, a fragment of sheet music.Nicolson is joined by fellow security operative Grant, and together they investigate the sudden appearance of the torpedo.
