Sontha Ooru (English: Native village) is a 2009 Telugu film starring Raja, Thirtha, L.B. Sriram in main roles. It was directed by P. Sunil Kumar Reddy. The film was screened at Several National & International film festivals like Mumbai Film Festival & Pune International Film Festival.[1][2] The film received four Nandi Awards.[3]Sontha Ooru is a film about rural life and how the innocent villagers are continuously exploited by the development plans of the government.
