Bobby Yeah is a British stop motion animated short horror film written, directed and animated by Robert Morgan. It was made independently and completed in 2011.The film premiered at the London International Animation Festival,[1] and went on to screen at numerous international film festivals, including The Sundance Film Festival.[2]Bobby Yeah was nominated for the 2012 BAFTA Award for Best Short Animation.[3]The film tells the story of Bobby Yeah - a subhuman creature who experiences a series of increasingly bizarre, nightmarish encounters after stealing a malevolent baby's favourite pet.
