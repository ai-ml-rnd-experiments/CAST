The Willies is a 1990 American comedy horror anthology film written and directed by Brian Peck.The film features cameos by several recognizable genre and character actors. James Karen and Clu Gulager, appear. Sean Astin plays one of the film's storytellers. Kathleen Freeman plays an elementary school teacher. Michael Bower plays a disturbed teenager. Kirk Cameron, Tracey Gold, and Jeremy Miller make appearances as does actress Chelsea Noble. Dana Ashbrook and Kimmy Robertson also appear.
