Harbour Beat is a 1990 Scottish-Australian film which marked the directorial debut of David Elfick.[2]Glasgow cop Neal McBride teams up with Australian cop Lancelot Cooper.$1.4 million of the budget came from the Film Finance Corporation.[3]The film was never released theatrically and debuted directly on television.
