Notting Hill is a 1999 romantic comedy film directed by Roger Michell. The screenplay was written by Richard Curtis, and the film was produced by Duncan Kenworthy. The film stars Julia Roberts, Hugh Grant, Rhys Ifans, Emma Chambers, Tim McInnerny, Gina McKee, and Hugh Bonneville. The story is of a romance between a London bookseller (Grant) and a famous American actress (Roberts), who happens to walk into his shop.Released on 21 May 1999, Notting Hill was well-received by critics and became the highest-grossing British film of all time.
