Cyrus, longer title Cyrus: Mind of a Serial Killer is a 2010 thriller horror film based on real events, written and directed by Mark Vadik and starring Brian Krause, Lance Henriksen and Danielle Harris.The film shows an independent television reporter and her cameraman, part of a small independent news crew interviewing a man in regards to a serial killer the man knew by the name of 'Cyrus' dubbed 'The County Line Cannibal'.
