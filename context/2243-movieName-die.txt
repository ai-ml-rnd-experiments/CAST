Die is a 2010 Canadian-Italian thriller film written by Domenico Salvaggio and directed by Dominic James and starring Elias Koteas and Emily Hampshire.[2][3][4]Six strangers wake up in cells in an underground facility. Their captive decides their fate with the roll of a die.
