Girls to Marry (German: Mädchen zum Heiraten) is a 1932 German romantic comedy film directed by Wilhelm Thiele and starring Renate Müller, Hermann Thimig and Wolf Albach-Retty.[1]The film's sets were designed by the art director Hans Jacoby. It was remade the same year in Britain as Marry Me, also directed by Wilhelm Thiele, with Müller starring again.This article related to a German film of the 1930s is a stub.
