Hit and Run is a 2012 American action comedy film written by Dax Shepard, with David Palmer and Shepard co-directing again (their first film being Brother's Justice in 2010). The film stars Shepard and his now-wife Kristen Bell, with Kristin Chenoweth, Tom Arnold, and Bradley Cooper. It was released on August 22, 2012.Charlie Bronson (Dax Shepard) is enrolled in Witness Protection, living in Milton, California under the supervision of incompetent U.S. Marshal Randy Anderson (Tom Arnold). Charlie's girlfriend Annie Bean (Kristen Bell) is a professor with a doctorate in Non-Violent Conflict Resolution. Her supervisor Debby Kreeger (Kristin Chenoweth) promises to fire her if she does not make it to a job interview with the University of California in Los Angeles.
