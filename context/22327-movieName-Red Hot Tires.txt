Red Hot Tires (1925) is a silent film comedy produced and released by Warner Brothers. The film was based on a story by Darryl Zanuck, under the name Gregory Rogers, and directed by Erle C. Kenton. The film stars Monte Blue and Patsy Ruth Miller. A copy is preserved in the Library of Congress collection.[1][2][3]A Warner Bros. film with the same title was made in 1935 but appears to be unrelated in story.
