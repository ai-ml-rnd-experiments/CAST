"Children of the Gods" is the first episode of the military science fiction television series Stargate SG-1. It was written by producers Jonathan Glassner and Brad Wright and was directed by Mario Azzopardi. The episode first aired on July 27, 1997 on Showtime. Originally presented as a television movie, the episode would later be split into two parts for repeats and syndicated viewings.
