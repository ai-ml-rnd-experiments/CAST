Polish Management or Polish Economy (German: Polnische Wirtschaft) is a 1928 German propaganda silent film directed by E. W. Emo and starring Iwa Wanja, Margot Landa, and Hans Brausewetter. It was one of a number of films attacking Poland's ownership of lands in which ethnic Germans lived during the 1920s.[1]The film's art direction was by Kurt Richter.This article related to a German silent film is a stub.
