Avanti! is a 1972 comedy film produced and directed by Billy Wilder. It stars Jack Lemmon and Juliet Mills, with Clive Revill, Edward Andrews, Gianfranco Barra, Franco Angrisano and Pippo Franco insupporting roles. The screenplay by Wilder and I. A. L. Diamond is based on Samuel A. Taylor's play, which had a short run on Broadway in 1968.[1] The film was shot on-location on the Italian island of Ischia and in the city of Sorrento.
