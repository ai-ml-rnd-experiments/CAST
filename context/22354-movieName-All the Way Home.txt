All the Way Home
(1960 play)
by Tad MoselAll the Way Home is a 1963 drama film directed by Alex Segal and starring Jean Simmons, Robert Preston, Pat Hingle, and Michael Kearney. The plot is about a young boy and his mother dealing with the sudden death of his father. It was based on the 1957 James Agee novel A Death in the Family[3] and the 1960 Tad Mosel play All the Way Home.[4]
