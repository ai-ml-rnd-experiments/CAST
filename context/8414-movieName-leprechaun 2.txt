Leprechaun 2 (also known as One Wedding and Lots of Funerals) is a 1994 American fantasy-horror-comedy film and the second film in the Leprechaun series. Directed by Rodman Flender, the film centers on a psychopathic leprechaun (Warwick Davis) hunting for a bride.Leprechaun 2 was released on April 8, 1994 and grossed $2.3 million at the box office. The film was critically panned upon release. It was the last in the series to receive a theatrical release.It is not clear if the leprechaun is the same as in the first film or are that film's other characters seen or mentioned.A sequel, Leprechaun 3 (1995), was released direct-to-video the following year.
