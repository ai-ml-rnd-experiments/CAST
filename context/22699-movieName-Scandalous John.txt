Scandalous John is a 1971 American comedy-drama film directed by Robert Butler and produced by Walt Disney Productions. It stars Brian Keith and Alfonso Arau.[1] The music was scored by Rod McKuen.John McCanless was once a gunslinger and has become a cranky old rancher. There is a plan to build a dam which would flood his property.
