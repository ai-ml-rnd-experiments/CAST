Slaughtered Vomit Dolls is a 2006 surrealist exploitation psychological horror film written and directed by Lucifer Valentine, who coined "vomit gore" as a new subgenre.[1] The film had a simultaneous DVD and limited theatrical release on February 14, 2006. The film follows a very loose plot structure, consisting of assorted random scenes mostly revolving around Angela Aberdeen, a bulimic stripper-turned-prostitute.Slaughtered Vomit Dolls is a part of the Vomit Gore Trilogy and was followed by two sequels: ReGOREgitated Sacrifice (2008) and Slow Torture Puke Chamber (2010). A fourth "Vomit Gore" film - titled Vomit Gore 4: Black Mass of the Nazi Sex Wizard - was released in 2015. Most recently, in May of 2020, an anthology film titled "The Angela Chapters" was released.Angela Aberdeen (Ameara LaVey) is a teenage runaway that suffers from bulimia. In order to make ends meet, she begins working as a prostitute.
