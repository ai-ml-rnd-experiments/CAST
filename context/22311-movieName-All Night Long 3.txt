All Night Long 3 (オールナイトロング3 最終章, All Night Long 3 Saishu Sho) is a 1996 Japanese original video horror film directed by Katsuya Matsumura, released on August 9, 1996.[1]Of Midnight Eye, Tom Mes said the film is "the best realised [of the trilogy] and the most relentless".[2]
