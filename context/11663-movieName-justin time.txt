Justin Time is a 2010 American direct-to-DVD action teen film. It was released on DVD on March 9, 2010.[1][2][3]An ancient and magical amulet that has the power to stop time is passed through generations until it ends up in the hands of a young man who must choose to use its power for good or for evil.
