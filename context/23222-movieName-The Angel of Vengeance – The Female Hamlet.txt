The Angel of Vengeance – The Female Hamlet (Turkish: İntikam Meleği – Kadın Hamlet) is a 1977 Turkish drama film directed by Metin Erksan. It was entered into the 10th Moscow International Film Festival.[1]This article related to a Turkish film is a stub.
