The Ladies Diplomat (German: Der Frauendiplomat) is a 1932 German comedy film directed by E. W. Emo and starring Mártha Eggerth, Max Hansen, and Leo Slezak.[1] It was remade in Britain in 1934 as The Diplomatic Lover.When a womanising military attaché arrives in Berlin he becomes entangled with a down-to-earth chorus girl Hella.This article related to a German film of the 1930s is a stub.
