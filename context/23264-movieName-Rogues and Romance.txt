Rogues and Romance is a surviving 1920 American silent drama film directed by George B. Seitz.[1] The film was a feature-length version of the serial Pirate Gold, also directed by Seitz, and was shot in Europe.[2] The film survives incomplete in the Library of Congress collection and George Eastman House Motion Picture Collection.[3][4]This article about a 1920s romantic drama film is a stub.
