Jake's Corner is a 2008 American independent drama film starring Richard Tyson and Danny Trejo.[1] The title of the film refers to the census-designated place of the same name.[2]The film was shot in three weeks in Jakes Corner, Arizona.[2]Laurie Jayne Frost of White Mountain Independent gave the film a positive review and wrote, "go see this movie."[1] Randy Cordova of The Arizona Republic gave the film a negative review and wrote: "The lunkheaded logic isn't made any more palatable by the script, in which all of Dunn's pals are given silly quirks and blah back stories."[3]This article about an independent drama film is a stub.
