Sirivantha is a 2006 Indian Kannada film starring Vishnuvardhan. This film was directed by S. Narayan.It was the sixth and last film of the Vishnu-Narayan combination. Sirivantha followed the other 5 Vishnu-Narayan films Veerappa Nayaka, Surya Vamsha, Simhadriya Simha, Jamindaru and Varsha. The movie is a remake of 2004 Telugu movie Aa Naluguru.It ran successfully for 100 days at the box office.[1]Sirivantha revolves around the concept of ideologies and being self-sufficient in life.
