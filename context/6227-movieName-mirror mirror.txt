Mirror Mirror is a 2012 American fantasy comedy film based on the fairy tale "Snow White" collected by the Brothers Grimm.It is directed by Tarsem Singh and produced by Ryan Kavanaugh, Bernie Goldmann, Brett Ratner and Kevin Misher. It was written by Marc Klein and Jason Keller, with music by Alan Menken. It stars Lily Collins, Julia Roberts, Armie Hammer, Nathan Lane, Mare Winningham, Michael Lerner, and Sean Bean.[3] It was released theatrically by Relativity Media on March 30, 2012.The film received an Academy Award nomination for Best Costume Design and earned $183 million on an $85-100 million budget but received generally mixed reviews from critics.[1] Mirror Mirror was released on DVD and Blu-ray by 20th Century Fox Home Entertainment on June 26, 2012.[4]A widowed king marries an evil witch named Clementianna, the most beautiful woman in the land.
