Bhoomi Geetha (Kannada: ಭೂಮಿಗೀತ, Song of the earth) is a 1997 award-winning Indian Kannada language film, starring Atul Kulkarni and Vinaya Prasad in lead roles.[1]The film focuses on the need for a balanced approach towards environment and tribal cultures that get displaced in the course of development.Filmfare Awards for KannadaKarnataka State Film Awards 1997-98National Film Awards 1997
