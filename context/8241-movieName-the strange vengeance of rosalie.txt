The Strange Vengeance of Rosalie is a 1972 thriller film directed by Jack Starrett and starring Bonnie Bedelia and Ken Howard.[1][2]A traveling salesman is lured by a precocious teenage girl to her shack in the desert for some sexual escapades. A scuzzy biker comes along, and they both find themselves dominated and tormented by him.
