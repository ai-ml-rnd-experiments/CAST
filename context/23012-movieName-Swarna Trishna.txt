Swarna Trisha is a 1990 Indian Bengali-language film directed by Mangal Chakraborty, starring Mithun Chakraborty, Prem Chopra, Anil Chatterjee, Tom Alter and Yogeeta Bali. The film also stars Hiro Kaju Kana Zowa, Hisa-Co-Kate and Sagiri Suzuki.Swarna Trisha is an action film starring Mithun Chakraborty in the lead role.
