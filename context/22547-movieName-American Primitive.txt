American Primitive is a play by William Gibson about the lives of John and Abigail Adams. Gibson used the correspondence of John and Abigail Adams to create a verse drama about the period of the American Revolution.American Primitive debuted, unsuccessfully, at the Berkshire Theatre Festival in 1969.
