The Fickle (Persian title: Bolhavass- Persian: بوالهوس‎) is a 1934 Iranian romance drama film directed by Ebrahim Moradi and starring Mohammad Ali Ghotbi, Ahmad Dehghan, Ahmad Gorji , Qodsi Partowi and Touran Veisi.[1]This article related to an Iranian film is a stub.
