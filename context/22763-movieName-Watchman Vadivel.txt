Watchman Vadivel is a 1994 Indian Tamil film, directed by A. Jagannathan and produced by Raadhika Reddy. The film stars Sivakumar, Sujatha, Anand Babu and Kasthuri in the lead roles. The film had musical score by Deva.[1][2] The film was a remake of the Telugu film Surigaadu.[3]Vadivel works as a watchman in a club. His wife Saro is a housewife. Both are doting parents to their only son, Raja who is a college student.
