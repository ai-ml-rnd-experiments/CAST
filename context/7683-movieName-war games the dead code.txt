WarGames: The Dead Code is a 2008 American direct-to-video thriller film written by Randall Badat and Rob Kerchner and directed by Stuart Gillard. It is the sequel to the 1983 film WarGames. Production began on November 20, 2006 in Montreal, and the film was released on DVD on July 29, 2008 by MGM's home video distributor 20th Century Fox Home Entertainment. According to MGM's original press release, the film is budgeted as one in a series of direct-to-DVD sequels.[1]Will Farmer's (Matt Lanter) neighbor Massude asks Will for help with a computer problem.
