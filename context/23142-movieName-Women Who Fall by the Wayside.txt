Women Who Fall by the Wayside (German: Frauen, die vom Weg abirren) is a 1925 German silent drama film directed by Géza von Bolváry and starring Ellen Kürti, Olaf Fjord, and Carl Walther Meyer.[1]It was made by Bavaria Film at the company's Emelka Studios in Munich.
