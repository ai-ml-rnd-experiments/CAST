In High Places (Italian:Quartieri alti) is a 1943 Italian comedy film directed by Mario Soldati and starring Adriana Benetti, Massimo Serato and Nerio Bernardi.[1] It is based on a play by Jean Anouilh. The film portrays the rise into high society of an immoral young man.[2]
