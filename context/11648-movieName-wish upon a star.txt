Wish Upon a Star is a 1996 television film directed by Blair Treu, written by Jessica Barondes, and starring Katherine Heigl and Danielle Harris. The film focuses on two teenage sisters who magically swap bodies after wishing on a shooting star. The sisters spend several days living each other's lives, sometimes attempting to sabotage the other's reputation and achievements. Eventually, they both learn to appreciate and help each other along the way. The tagline of the movie is "I Wish I May, I Wish I Might, Become My Sister For A Night!".[1]Alexia and Hayley Wheaton are sisters who share a home and a high school, but feel that they have little in common. Eighteen-year-old Alexia (Katherine Heigl) is popular, dresses in style, has a jock boyfriend, Kyle (Don Jeffcoat), and puts no effort into schoolwork. In contrast, her fifteen-year-old sister Hayley (Danielle Harris) is socially reserved and admires her older sister's popularity from a distance, while excelling in her studies, particularly in science and mathematics.
