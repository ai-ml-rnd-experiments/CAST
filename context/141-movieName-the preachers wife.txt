The Preacher's Wife is a 1996 American comedy-drama film directed by Penny Marshall and starring Denzel Washington, Whitney Houston, and Courtney B. Vance. It is a remake of the 1947 film The Bishop's Wife,[2] which in turn was based on the novel of the same name by Robert Nathan.It was nominated for the Oscar for Best Music, Original Musical or Comedy Score.
