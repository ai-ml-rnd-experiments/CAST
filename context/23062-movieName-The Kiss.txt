Kiss a 2019 Kannada-language romantic movie directed by A. P. Arjun. The film was produced by V. Ravi Kumar under his home banner Rashtrakuta Pictures. This was the debut movie for both the lead actors Viraat and Sree Leela.[1] Actress Mayuri Kyatari provided dubs for Shree Leela's voice.[2] Kiss is the second Kannada-language movie after A 2nd Hand Lover (2015) to be inspired by the 2004 South Korean film 100 Days with Mr.
