Ride the Wild Surf is a 1964 American romantic drama film. It was filmed in 1963 and distributed in 1964. Unlike the beach party movies of the era, this was a departure from the typical Hollywood approach to surfing as it was a drama, not a comedy. It is known for its exceptional big wave surf footage – a common sight in surf movies of the time, but a rarity in Hollywood films.[2] Likewise, the film has only one pop song – the titular Jan and Dean track, which is heard once, at the end of the film.Tom Lisanti wrote the movie "stands head and shoulders above all the sixties beach party movies...
