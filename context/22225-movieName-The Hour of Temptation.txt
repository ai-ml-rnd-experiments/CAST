The Hour of Temptation (German: Die Stunde der Versuchung) is a 1936 German mystery film directed by Paul Wegener and starring Gustav Fröhlich, Lída Baarová and Harald Paulsen.[1]The film's sets were designed by Karl Machus and Otto Moldenhauer.This article related to a German film of the 1930s is a stub.
